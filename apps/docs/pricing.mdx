## Plans

VN MCP uses a simple call-based pricing model. Pay in USD via Stripe or in VND via MoMo.

| Plan | Price (USD) | Price (VND) | Calls/month | Servers |
|------|-------------|-------------|-------------|---------|
| **Free** | $0 | Free | 1,000 | 2 servers |
| **Starter** | $19/mo | 449,000 VND/mo | 10,000 | All 5 servers |
| **Pro** | $49/mo | 1,190,000 VND/mo | 100,000 | All 5 servers |
| **Business** | $149/mo | 3,590,000 VND/mo | Unlimited | All 5 servers + priority support |

<Note>Free tier includes 2 servers — MoMo and ZaloPay. Upgrade to access VNPAY, Zalo OA, and ViettelPay.</Note>

- Sandbox/mock mode only
- Community support

Best for: Prototyping and personal projects.

## FAQ

**What counts as an API call?**

Each tool invocation counts as one call. For example, calling `momo_create_payment` once uses 1 call.

**What happens when I hit my limit?**

API calls return a `429 Too Many Requests` error. Upgrade your plan in the dashboard to restore access immediately.

**Can I switch plans?**

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time from the [dashboard](https://dashboard.mcpvn.dev). Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the next billing cycle.

**Is there an annual discount?**

Not currently. Annual pricing is planned for a future release.
